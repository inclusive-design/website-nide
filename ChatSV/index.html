ATRC Self Voicing Chat

Introduction

The ATRC Self Voicing Chat is a Java IRC client designed to work with IBM's Self Voicing Kit (SVK). Users who have the SVK installed on their computer will be able to hear text-to-speech of the messages being exchanged during a chat session.

The ATRC Self Voicing Chat is a project of The Adaptive Technology Resource Centre at the University Of Toronto.
